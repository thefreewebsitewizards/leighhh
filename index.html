My journey as an author is shaped by obedience and formed through restoration, not quick answers. About the Author

About Leigh

My path to becoming an author, particularly focusing on faith and reflection, was forged in seasons of profound challenge. Through illness, loss, and deeply personal trials, I learned to release control and trust God beyond my understanding.

These transformative periods, though difficult, revealed that healing is often God's unseen work, restoring what feels broken and renewing what seems lost. Reflection became prayer, and journaling became worship.

"I write to share the restoring power of Yeshua's love."

Healing is possible, restoration is real, and God is at work, even in the waiting. My words aim to remind you that your current season is not the end of your story.

Honoring Scripture while speaking to the lived experiences of the heart.

The Message

The heart of my message

My writing is both reflective and declarative, born from seasons of healing and restoration, and speaking with conviction about God's faithfulness. I approach faith not as theory, but as testimony refined through endurance.

I want readers to experience that God’s promises are living, active, and trustworthy. While I invite personal examination, I also boldly declare that restoration is real, waiting is purposeful, and Yeshua remains faithful in every season.

My perspective offers a reminder that faith is not passive; it calls us to trust, surrender, and stand firmly in God’s Word. My hope is to not only comfort but strengthen you, encouraging belief in the God who restores and fulfills every promise.

"Restoration is real, waiting is purposeful, and Yeshua remains faithful."
